Best Hiking Trails in Southwest Ohio

Explore scenic river valleys, historic earthworks, and diverse trails in Southwest Ohio's Cincinnati region

Discover Southwest Ohio's Scenic River Valleys and Historic Trails

Southwest Ohio's hiking landscape is defined by the beautiful Little Miami River valley, ancient Native American earthworks, and diverse terrain ranging from gentle paved trails to challenging gorge hikes. The Cincinnati region offers excellent access to state parks, nature preserves, and one of Ohio's premier scenic trails, perfect for hikers of all skill levels.

Southwest Ohio Hiking Tips

Historic Sites

Southwest Ohio features numerous ancient Native American earthworks and historic sites. Visit Fort Ancient and other archaeological sites to combine hiking with cultural history education.

River Trails

The Little Miami Scenic Trail is perfect for long-distance hiking or biking. Access points throughout the trail allow for shorter segments, and multiple towns offer services along the route.

Scenic Valleys

Fall colors are spectacular in Southwest Ohio's river valleys, typically peaking mid-October. Caesar Creek Gorge and East Fork State Park offer especially beautiful autumn hiking.
