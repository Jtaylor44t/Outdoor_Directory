Discover Northwest Ohio's Unique Trail Experiences

Northwest Ohio offers a hiking experience unlike anywhere else in the state. From the rare sand dune ecosystems of Oak Openings to the peaceful wetlands along Lake Erie, this region showcases diverse natural habitats and excellent trail systems managed by the Toledo area Metroparks.

Northwest Ohio Hiking Tips

Birdwatching Paradise

Northwest Ohio lies along a major migration route. Bring binoculars especially in spring and fall to spot warblers, eagles, and waterfowl along Lake Erie.

Insect Protection

Wetland trails can have mosquitoes and ticks, especially May-September. Use insect repellent and check for ticks after hiking in tall grass areas.

Sand Dune Footwear

Trails in Oak Openings feature deep sand in some areas. Wear supportive hiking shoes rather than sandals for better traction and comfort.
